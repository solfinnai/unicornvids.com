The Brief

Launch a premium rum brand from one of the most famous people on Earth. Make it feel like a million bucks.

Three Hearts is Johnny Depp's premium rum brand — not a celebrity endorsement deal, but a brand he actually created. The team needed launch content for Instagram that would establish Three Hearts as a serious player in the premium spirits space from day one.

The challenge: compete visually with Depp's Dior Sauvage campaigns — productions that run into the millions — but do it on a fraction of the budget. The content had to feel premium, intimate, and unmistakably Three Hearts. Not a fragrance ad cosplay. Something original.

The Guerrilla Approach

We shot the whole thing in a living room. On purpose.

No studio rental. No location permits. No catering truck. We transformed a living room into a premium set — controlled lighting, carefully art-directed compositions, and a production approach built on the belief that constraints breed creativity.

Total production cost: under $10,000. That's not a typo. Where a traditional agency would have quoted six figures for a celebrity brand launch of this magnitude, we delivered two polished Instagram films that look like they cost 20x what they did.

The Results

Instagram numbers that rival a Dior campaign.

The Three Hearts launch videos hit Instagram and immediately performed at a level comparable to Depp's Dior Sauvage content — campaigns backed by one of the largest luxury advertising budgets in the world. Views, engagement rate, and audience sentiment all tracked at parity with the highest-end partnerships on his profile.

More importantly, the content successfully launched Three Hearts as a premium brand. The feedback from both the team and the audience was overwhelmingly positive. The videos didn't just perform well — they set the visual identity and tone for the entire brand going forward.

Your Playbook

What Three Hearts proves about modern production.

01

Constraints are a creative advantage

A living room forced us to be intentional about every frame. No wasted shots, no filler. Every creative decision was driven by the brand, not the budget. The limitation became the signature.

02

Premium is a feeling, not a price tag

Dior spends millions because they can, not because they have to. Lighting, composition, pacing, and art direction create the feeling of premium. Those are skills, not line items.

03

Speed kills (in a good way)

Traditional agency timelines for a celebrity brand launch: 6-12 weeks. We delivered in days. When you strip away the layers of bureaucracy and bloat, creative moves faster and hits harder.

04

The audience doesn't see your budget

Nobody watching the Three Hearts videos on Instagram knows they cost under $10K. They see the brand, the craft, the vibe. The numbers prove it — audiences respond to quality, not spend.
